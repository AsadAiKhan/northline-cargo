About North Line Cargo L.L.C.

We are a leading logistics company based in Sharjah, UAE, specializing in international cargo transportation and supply chain solutions.

Founded with a vision to connect businesses across continents, North Line Cargo has established itself as a trusted partner in the logistics industry. Our team of experienced professionals brings decades of combined experience in cargo handling, customs clearance, and international shipping.

We pride ourselves on delivering reliable, efficient, and cost-effective logistics solutions to our clients across the UAE, Africa, and beyond.

Mr. Mohammad Akram - Founder & CEO

With over 20 years of experience in the logistics industry, Mr. Akram brings a wealth of knowledge and vision to North Line Cargo.

Under his leadership, the company has grown from a regional player to an international logistics powerhouse, serving clients across multiple continents.

His commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction drives the company's continuous improvement and expansion.
